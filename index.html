My Running History

I started running 3 years ago, when I became tired of being out of shape. I fell in love with it instantly.

My first long race was the Indy Mini Marathon in May of 2019. After this, I started training for the Indy Monumental Marathon in November of 2019. during some runs, I started to feel a pain in my right knee. I didn't think too much of it because the pain wasn't too bad and I could run on it. Duing a 12 mile long run, the pain was too much and I had to stop running. I attempted running most days, but I couldn't get past 1-2 miles without having to stop.

After attempting to run on my own for two months, I finally decided to see a doctor who specialized in running injuries. I went through two rounds of therapy with different therapists to work through my pain.

I am finally in a place where I can start to slowly build up my mileage while also working on strength training to prevent injuries. My plans are documented below.
